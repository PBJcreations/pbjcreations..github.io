Doodle a Day Challenge

This was a part of my Febraury (2020) hobby challenge where I drew one doodle a day over the span of 30 days (A few more extra after February to end on a good number). I love to learn new hobbies and skills however the workload between my college courses at UNO and my two part time jobs doing student research took higher priority. The reason for this challenge was to force myself to create a habit for learning a new skill to prove to myself that I've just been making excuses not to start.
