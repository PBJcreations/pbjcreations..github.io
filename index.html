About Me

Bio

Parker Jensen is currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at the University of Nebraska, Omaha (UNO). He has been involved in multiple organizations on campus such as planning student events with Maverick Productions or inspiring others' creativity as the President of UNO Maker Group. Parker currently works as a student engineering technician at the Biomechanics Research Building at UNO to design and fabricate prototypes for clients including medical devices, virtual reality software, and mechanical parts for larger projects. Parker is very grateful for his opportunities and eagerly waits for what is next. In his free time, you can find Parker woodworking, camping, drawing, or socializing with friends and family.

About This Website

Learning HTML

This website was created to showcase my projects from work, school, and my personal hobbies to my friends, family, and potential employers. Since many may know me as one that likes to try new things (For instance: learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to program this website!) this website helps me organize and reflect on the various skills and accomplishments along my journey.
